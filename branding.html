BRIANNE DOMINIQUE PETRONE

Show Must Go On


Services: Photography, Editing, Creative Direction.


I had the incredible honor of working with my close friend, creative soulmate, and the wildly talented independent artist, AVE. Her most recent album titled, Show Must Go On, released in January 2022, is a collection of songs advocating for social issues through soulful storytelling lyrics and immersive sounds. Each track on the album covers a different social issue, and its order coincides with the month of awareness associated with each song.

With themes of empathy, equality, and empowerment, our goal with this shoot was to embody the essense of the phrase "the show must go on," a mantra that AVE has come to live by. She came to the table with the idea for a stage shoot, featuring theater seats, curtains, and spotlights, matching the theatricality, power, and drama of this record.

This shoot not only resulted in the rebrand of AVE for this new show-stopping era, but it also resulted in the album cover art for Show Must Go On.
