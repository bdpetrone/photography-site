Show Must Go On


I had the incredible honor of working with my close friend, creative soulmate, and wildly talented independent artist, AVE. Her most recent album titled, Show Must Go On, released in January 2022, is a collection of songs advocating for social issues through soulful storytelling lyrics and immersive sounds. Each track on the album covers a different social issue, and its order coincides with the month of awareness associated with each song.

With themes of empathy, equality, and empowerment, our goal with this shoot was to embody the essense of the phrase "the show must go on," a mantra that AVE has come to live by. AVE came to the table with a stage shoot concept, featuring theater seats, curtains, and spotlights, to match the theatricality, power, and drama of this record.

The shoot not only resulted in the rebrand of AVE for this new show-stopping era, but also resulted in the album cover art for Show Must Go On.

Triple Threat


A new album = a new era, and I had the pleasure, once again, of helping bring the visuals to life. AVE's EP, Triple Threat, a collection of lyrically-driven, danceable pop songs, was released in March of 2023.

Bringing back the best of the early 2000s, the decade of CDs, bright colors, paparazzi, and tabloid-culture, we had lots to take inspiration from. This Y2K pop rebrand took insights from icons like Britney, Christina, JLo, and more.

Producing promotional materials, social posts, and cover art, we built a world reminiscint of this time period, while making it original to AVE; a true triple threat.
