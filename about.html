BRIANNE DOMINIQUE PETRONE

A bit about me...


Hi! My name's Brianne Petrone.

I'm a freelance photographer based outside of Westchester, New York. I specialize in portrait, stock, and travel photography.

I believe in the power of visual storytelling as an incredible form of art and communication. My goal as a photographer is to capture people and places in their unique essence.

Intentionality and experience are at the forefront of my services. Care and attention to detail are a priority in every shoot. Collaboration and positive energy are what you can expect, as I aim to make our time together as comfortable, enjoyable, and confidence-inducing as possible.

With experience on both sides of the lens, I'm all for helping out with posing and direction. I view every shoot as a collaboration, a fun hang out, and a creative adventure. Whether you have ideas to bring to the table or need some direction, expect a shoot that leaves you feeling refreshed and inspired!

Let's collaborate!
